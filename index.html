About Me

Hello, and thank you for visiting my website.

I’m Manish Vaishnav, a finance professional with over two decades of experience in managing financial operations, leading teams, and driving strategic initiatives across diverse industries. Currently serving as Director of Finance & Administration at a technology-driven company in Singapore, I have built my career on strong financial acumen, hands-on leadership, and a deep understanding of international financial reporting standards (IFRS).

Beyond the spreadsheets and boardrooms, I’m also a family man, a lifelong learner, and someone who strongly believes in living by values.

Learning is a continuous journey for me. I love reading as it helps me learn and grow every day. Whether it's a timeless classic or a new perspective from a modern author, books are one of my greatest teachers.

I’m deeply curious about a wide range of subjects beyond finance—from spirituality, psychology, and neuroscience to technology, artificial intelligence, and data analytics. I’m fascinated by how these disciplines intersect and influence the way we live, work, and think.

This website is a small step towards sharing ideas, connecting with like-minded individuals, and giving back through the knowledge I’ve gained over the years.
